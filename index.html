About Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is a fantasy action RPG and gachapon game available on PC, mobile, and Playstation! 🌟 The game is about a traveler from another world (you) on a journey to find your long-lost sibling, battling monsters 🏹, and discovering the mysteries of Teyvat, and yourself along the way.

About This Club

Since Genshin Impact allows for co-op multiplayer options across the various platforms, it allows for travelers to help each other with various tasks and adventures. This club is a space that provides information, tips, and a way to connect with players in the Bay Area.
